Monopoly Instructions

- How To Play

To play the game, simply click the buttons on screen as
they appear in accordance with what action you want to
perform. The goal of the game is to maximize the number
of properties you own, as well as the amount of money you
have.

Read more about the rules below.

- http://richard_wilding.tripod.com/monorules.htm

- Interacting With The GUI

The GUI has various elements that can be used to enhance
the experience that otherwise may not be super apparent.

  • Click on the top left corner of the in-game window (where
    it says "Player #") and a new GUI will appear. This 'Player
    Stats' window gives more detailed information about each
    player and the properties they own.

  • When you own a property (which is indicated by the color
    that matches the current player's color) you can left
    click on it to display additional options including the
    ability to mortage a property and add/remove upgrades
    from each property. This menu is only accessable when
    the "Roll" button is on the screen.
